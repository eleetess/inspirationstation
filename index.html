What to expect

  • Exquisite Corpse-Creative Writing:Take a line from favorite movie or book and rewrite the text to change the storyline completely.example: She was warned to not open the book but she started to read anyway, what she found surprised her.
  • Earth watercolors-collect different forms of clay and dirt from outside, mix with water til you reach your desired consistency and let your imagination do the rest.
  • Floral Jewels-make necklaces,rings and more out of flowers
  • Mad libs-Take different story lines and characters and do a mad lib version to tell a new story
  • Live game board-Create your own game board using left over boxes, containers etc.
  • Chalk Walk- draw different path ways on the road or side walk to give an unexpected travel plans to your daily walk
  • Remix Master-take two songs, play one song with the instrumental only using the words to another song
